Winning numbers for Friday, 4th of December, 2020 and more about the Mega Millions Megaplier!

8th December 2020 9:08 AM

Let’s take a look at last Friday’s USA Mega Millions winning numbers as well as further information about the Mega Millions Megaplier. With a $264 Million jackpot, things are getting quite exciting by the minute.

Mega Millions’ jackpot is growing as it now stands at the $264 Million mark. With yet another rollover, there was no jackpot winner, resulting in the Mega Millions jackpot to be up for grabs once more in Atlanta, Georgia on Tuesday, 8th of December at 23:00 ET. 

If you’re looking for the Mega Millions winning numbers results, check them out below:

Main Numbers - 3, 19, 24, 44, 50

Mega Ball - 8    Megaplier - x3

There were a total of 673,216 people who won a prize during the last Mega Millions drawing. One very lucky individual from that list won a whopping $1 Million second-tier prize after managing to match with the five main numbers. If this lucky winner had purchased the ticket with a megaplier, the win would’ve totaled to a colossal $3 Million. 13 other won $10,000 each after matching with four of the main numbers as well as the Mega Ball. Two out of the 13 winners had purchased their ticket with the Megaplier, which meant that their win was of $30,000. 

The next Mega Millions drawing will happen on Tuesday, 8th of December at 23:00 ET so make sure to purchase your tickets in time to have your spot saved, for a chance to win $264 Million.

What is the Mega Millions Megaplier?

A Mega Millions jackpot win would truly be indescribable but what if we were to tell you that your non-jackpot win could be doubled, tripled, quadrupled, and even quintupled! Whilst you’re purchasing your Mega Millions tickets, simply tick the Megaplier and you’re all set for a potential surprising victory.

The Megaplier is something you can add to your Mega Millions ticket, and things will just continue to get exciting from there onwards. Say you win the second prize from the prize tier - which is $1 Million - and the Megaplier is at the x4 mark; this way you would’ve won $4 Million big ones if your ticket included the Megaplier. The Megaplier comes with an additional cost to your ticket, however, it’s definitely worth a shot for a chance to double your winnings.
