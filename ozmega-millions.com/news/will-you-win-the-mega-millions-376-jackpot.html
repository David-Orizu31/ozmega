Will you be the one to win the Mega Millions $376M jackpot?

28th December 2020 2:09 PM

This week, we’ll leave the year 2020 behind and start the new year of 2021, so why don’t you try your hand on the massive USA Mega Millions $376 jackpot? Read below for more.

As we close off yet another chapter called 2020, Mega Millions is providing its players with the incredible chance of winning the $376,000,000 jackpot. Now that surely would be an unforgettable start of the new year.

The upcoming USA Mega Millions drawing will happen tomorrow 29th of December, at 11 PM so be certain that your spot is saved so you don’t miss your possible winning opportunity. 

To add, just in case you missed out on last Friday’s draw - the Mega Millions winning numbers results of the Christmas Day drawing were as follows:

Main Numbers - 17, 23, 36, 69 & 70

Mega Ball - 19  Megaplier - X2

This Christmas, there were 808,822 winning individuals who managed to win a jolly prize. Three people won $1 Million just by matching with the five main numbers. None of them had purchased their ticket with the Megaplier, therefore, their win was not duplicated to $2 Million. 17 others matched with the four main numbers as well as the Mega Ball and won $10,000 each. Two out of the 17 winners had purchased their ticket with the Megaplier, and their win was duplicated to $20,000 each. 442 won $500 each by matching with the four main numbers - 73 of which had their win duplicated due to the Megaplier and totaling their win to $1000 each. Check out the results page for more details in regards to the Mega Millions winners.

Furthermore, the Megaplier only costs an additional price of $1 in most states and could even provide you with either a doubled, tripled, quadrupled, or even a quintupled jackpot. Push your luck with a cheap additional price for a likely gigantic first prize. 

Ever since the Mega Millions jackpot was won on the 15th of September of this year in the state of Wisconsin, there’s been a total of 14,900,000 different winning tickets sold with various levels of prizes - 22 of which were of $1 Million and more.

This year of 2020, the Mega Millions jackpot was won five times in total. On the 9th of June, the state of Arizona experienced its very first jackpot win of $410 Million. Meanwhile, on the 15th of September, the state of Wisconsin also experienced its first-ever jackpot win of $120 Million. Another two jackpot wins occurred in the state of New Jersey - one on the 11th of February of $202 Million and one on the 24th of July of $123 Million. On the 31st of July, another win happened in the state of California of $22 Million.

Don’t miss out on this starry opportunity - the next draw will happen tomorrow night at 11 PM. Be sure to have your Mega Millions tickets purchased; who knows you might just win the entire $376 Million jackpot.
