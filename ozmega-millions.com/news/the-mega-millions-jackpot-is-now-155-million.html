The Mega Millions Jackpot is now $155 million

29th January 2020 11:12 AM

The draw of Tuesday, January 28 did not see any winners, so Mega Millions is now at an astonishing $155 million! Let’s have a look at last night’s results.

Latest Mega Millions Results

Nobody matched all five numbers together with the Mega Ball last night, so we now have a rollover. The previous jackpot was $141 million with a cash option of $98.6 million.

The winning numbers for the draw of Tuesday January 28, 2020 were:
17-36-47-51-62 - Mega Ball 21 - Megaplier 3X

The second tier prize was not claimed either since no one matched the five numbers. However, 12 people managed to match four numbers and the Mega Ball, each snatching an amazing $10,000 prize. One of them also won the Megaplier prize, making their pockets $30,000 heavier.

The Mega Millions rollover has landed us with a magnificent $155 million jackpot, with a cash option of $108.3 million.

The Last Mega Millions Jackpot Has Been Claimed

The winner of the last Mega Millions jackpot wished to remain anonymous and the prize was claimed by a legal trust. The draw took place on December 17, 2019 with a jackpot of $375 million, making it the largest Ohio Lottery prize ever.

The Great Hope Trust, whose trustee is listed as attorney Brian C. Mulhall, opted for the one-time lump-sum payment. This landed them with a $182,555,782 prize (post state and federal taxes).

The ticket was sold at the Giant Eagle in Mentor, Ohio, who received a bonus of $100,000 for selling the winning ticket. 

The next draw of the Mega Millions lottery will be taking place this Friday, January 31, at 23:00 EST, with a jackpot of $155 million.
