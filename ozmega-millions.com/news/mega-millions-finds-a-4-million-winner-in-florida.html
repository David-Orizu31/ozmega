Mega Millions Finds a $4 million Winner in Florida

23rd October 2019 2:20 PM

On the Mega Millions draw that took place on Tuesday October 22nd, there was a player in Florida who won $4 million thanks to the Megaplier option.

Latest Mega Millions Results

Here are the winning numbers for the $82 million Mega Millions drawn on Tuesday, October 22nd:
5-11-14-23-25 - Mega Ball 10 - Megaplier 4x

There were no winners who matched the five main numbers and the MegaBall to win the first-tier prize. However, there were 2 winning tickets which matched 5 balls, carrying the prize of $1 million. $4 million were also won by one of these players who had the Megaplier. The $1 million ticket was bought in New York whilst the $4 million ticket was bought in Florida.

Yesterday’s draw saw another 23 winners who matched four balls and the Mega Ball, winning them $10,000. Eight of these players also had the Megaplier, which made them winners of $40,000 each.

The Mega Millions jackpot has gone up to $93 million for the next draw.

Cape Girardeau winner claims $3 million Mega Millions prize

Missouri has claimed the largest non-jackpot Mega Millions prize ever won in the state. Cathy Blackwell from Cape Girardeau has claimed her $3 million Mega Millions prize when she turned in her winning ticket that she had bought from Schnucks Market early October. 

Blackwell’s ticket matched all 5 white ball numbers which won her $1 million, but since she added Megaplier to her play, her prize was tripled since the Megaplier was 3.

Both yesterday’s winner from Florida as well as the winner from Cape Girardeau won a bigger prize thanks to the Megaplier, the option that can multiply your winnings up to 5 times your original winning amount, costing only $1.
