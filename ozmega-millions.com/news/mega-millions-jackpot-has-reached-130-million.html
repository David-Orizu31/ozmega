Mega Millions Jackpot has Reached $130 million

22nd January 2020 12:12 PM

With the $116 million jackpot not having been won on the 21st January 2020, a rollover has landed us a new astounding jackpot of $130 million.

Latest Mega Millions Numbers

The winning numbers for the $116 million Mega Millions draw which took place on Tuesday, January 21st 2020 were:
16-18-29-31-37 - Mega Ball 8 - Megaplier 3X

Even though nobody matched all 5 numbers together with the Mega Ball in last night’s Mega Millions draw, meaning that there was no jackpot winner, a lucky individual who matched the 5 numbers is now $1,000,000 richer. This ticket was sold in Texas.

Not having a jackpot winner also means that we have a rollover. The Mega Millions jackpot has now reached an amazing $130 million.

Check out the full payouts of last night’s draw.

Last Mega Millions Jackpot Win Still Unclaimed

The Mega Millions draw of December 17th 2019 saw an astonishing $372 million being won by a single ticket-holder in Ohio. The winner, who purchased the ticket through the auto lotto feature, matched all 5 numbers and the Mega Ball. However, they still have not claimed the prize. Ohio winners have a total of 180 days to claim their prize; Mega Millions started in Ohio back in 2002, making this ticket the 20th Mega Millions jackpot ticket sold in the state.

The winning ticket was sold at a Giant Eagle supermarket in Mentor, meaning that Giant Eagle will also receive a bonus of $100,000 for selling the winning ticket.

Coincidentally, a huge jackpot of $648 million had also been won on the same date in a different year. On the 17th December 2013, winners from California and Georgia shared the staggering prize, which was the third-largest prize in the history of Mega Millions.

The next Mega Millions draw will be taking place on Friday, January 24th with a jackpot of $130 million.
