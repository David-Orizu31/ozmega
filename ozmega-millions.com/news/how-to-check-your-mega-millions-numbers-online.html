How to check your numbers online after purchasing your Mega Millions ticket for the $310 Million jackpot!

16th December 2020 1:00 PM

The USA Mega Millions $310 Million jackpot continues to grow and grow, so don’t miss your chance to win it all. The next drawing will take place this Friday at 23.00 EST. Read more.

Mega Millions’ jackpot just keeps getting bigger and better. Its jaw-dropping prize of $310 Million can be awarded by one lucky winner this Friday, 18th of December at 11 PM, so be sure to purchase your tickets in advance so that you save your spot within the mega draw.

In case you missed it, here are the most recent Mega Millions’ winning numbers results:

Main Numbers - 1, 10, 18, 20 & 46 

Mega Ball - 15  Megaplier - X2

In the latest Mega Millions drawing, there were 722,722 winners in total, and they each took a prize home. Three winners won $1 Million each by matching the five main numbers. None of them purchased their tickets with the Megaplier, therefore, their win did not duplicate itself. 28 other individuals matched with the four main numbers as well as the Mega Ball and scored a win of $10,000 each. Three of those 28 winners purchased their ticket with the Megaplier so their win totaled up to $20,000. 571 people matched with the four main numbers and won $500 each. 101 out of the 571 Mega Millions winners had their ticket with the Megaplier so they took home $1000. Feel like calling yourself a Mega Millions winner? Purchase your tickets before the exciting draw on Friday at 11 PM, to potentially get your hands on the massive $310 Million jackpot.

 

Checking your Mega Millions numbers online

In order for you to check your USA Mega Millions numbers online, all you will have to do is give our Mega Millions results page a look after any particular draw. This page is updated after each and every draw so that you’re always up to date with all the most recent draws.

If you’re curious about whether you’re due a prize, you can always compare your submitted numbers with the winning ones available on the Mega Millions results page. You can also input your numbers into our generator so that you can see if you’ve matched with any of the winning numbers.
