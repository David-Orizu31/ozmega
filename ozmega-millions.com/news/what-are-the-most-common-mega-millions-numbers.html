What Are the Most Common Mega Millions Numbers?

13th October 2017 11:40 AM

The Mega Millions jackpot for tonight is an estimated $43 million, after no players managed to win the top prize in five consecutive drawings. If you are unsure which numbers to pick, take a look at the useful statistics* on this page for some inspiration.  

What Are the Most Common Mega Millions Numbers? 

The top six most common Mega Millions numbers are 31, 2, 17, 39, 5, and 35. The number 31 has been drawn the most, emerging from the machine 207 times, most recently on September 9th, 2017. The numbers 2 and 17 have both been drawn 206 times, with 39 (205 times) following shortly behind. That ball was last drawn on September 22nd, 2017. 

The most common Mega Ball numbers are 3, 15, 7, 1, 10, and 9. The number 3 was last drawn 28 days ago and has been drawn a total of 84 times. Mega Ball 9 has been drawn the least out of this selection, and was last seen 119 days ago, on June 16th, 2017. 

What Are the Most Overdue Mega Millions Numbers? 

The most overdue Mega Millions numbers are 48, 43, 29, 69, 3, and 27. The number 48 is the most overdue, as it was last drawn 224 days ago, on March 3rd, 2017. The number 43 follows behind, as it was last drawn 164 days ago, on May 2nd, 2017. 

What Are the Latest Jackpot Winning Numbers?
 
The Mega Millions jackpot was last won on September 22nd and the value of the jackpot was $104 million. The winning numbers for the drawing were 5, 39, 54, 63, 66, and Mega Ball 15. There were a total of 1,331,640 prize winners in the drawing and the jackpot winner is yet to come forward to claim their prize. Details of the payouts and the location where the jackpot winning ticket was sold, can be found on the Mega Millions Numbers page.  

Where Can I Find More Mega Millions Statistics? 

If you are looking for more information, you can find a wealth of statistics on the game by visiting the Mega Millions Statistics section. You can view statistics, such as the most common pairs and the least often drawn numbers. 

Tonight’s drawing takes place at 23.00 EST, so there’s plenty of time for you to choose those all-important digits. You can visit your local authorized retailer in participating states or choose your numbers online.  

*Statistics include all draws up to and including October 11th, 2017.
