Play Mega Millions Online

Mega Millions
› Play Online

You can enter Mega Millions by picking your numbers online. Draws take place twice a week on Tuesday and Friday evenings at 23.00 ET. Make sure to choose your numbers before the draw closes to be in with the chance of winning a colossal prize.

Follow these steps to get started:

  • Select any of the 'Play Online' banners on mega-millions.com.
  • Choose your own numbers by filling in the online playslip or use the Quick Pick option.
  • If you are logged in then you can simply pay for your entries with your account balance.
  • If you are not logged in or don’t have an account, click Pay to create an account.
  • Fill in your details.
  • Select a payment method from the available options.
  • Proceed to the checkout and review your order.
  • Complete your purchase by selecting the 'Pay' option.
  • You will be notified by email if you win a prize and any winnings due will be paid into your online account.
  • If you win a prize, you can withdraw your winnings at any time or use them for future entries.

By choosing your numbers online, you can enjoy many benefits, such as:

  • You can choose your numbers at a time that suits you and you can save time by not having to visit a retailer.
  • You can choose your numbers using your mobile phone, tablet or on your computer.
  • You can play for more than one draw at a time so you will never miss another draw again.
  • You won’t miss out on any prizes due on winning entries, as your numbers are checked against the latest Mega Millions numbers and any prize winnings are automatically paid into your account.
  • You don’t have to worry about losing a ticket, as once you have picked your numbers, the information is available in your online account.
