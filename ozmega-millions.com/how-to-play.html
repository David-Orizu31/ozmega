How to Play Mega Millions

Mega Millions
› How to Play

Players are required to select five main numbers from 1 to 70 and one Mega Ball number from a separate pool of 1 to 25. If a player matches all six numbers drawn, they win the jackpot. If there are no jackpot winners, the top prize rolls over to the following drawing. The Mega Millions jackpot starts at $40 million and increases each time there is a rollover. As there is no jackpot cap, jackpots have the ability to roll into hundreds of millions of dollars.

Play Mega Millions Online

Playing online is simple and secure. Simply select the 'Play Now' banner above to pick your numbers now for the next Mega Millions draw and to be in with a chance of winning a huge prize. For step by step instructions on how to play Mega Millions, visit the Play Online page.

If you win a prize, you will be notified of your win via email and you can view the winnings in your online account. If you win the jackpot, a representative will contact you and guide you through the claims process.

Mega Millions Draws

Draws are held in Atlanta, Georgia every Tuesday and Friday at 23.00 EST. The cut-off time for buying entries closes at least an hour prior to the draw, so you should make sure to give yourself plenty of time to pick your numbers, especially when the jackpot is a large amount.

Comparison Between Playing Online and via a Retailer

Here are some of the differences of playing Mega Millions online vs at a retailer:

Play Online Play via a Retailer
Convenience Choose your numbers at any time without having to leave the comfort of your own home, taking the hassle out of a trip to the store. Tickets can only be purchased from authorised stores.
Flexibility You can choose you numbers at a time that suits you. Store opening hours are limited and tickets can only be purchased during opening hours.
Security Once you have chosen and paid for your entries, they are safely stored in your online account, eliminating the risk of losing or damaging tickets. Your ticket is your only proof of purchase and if anything happens to your ticket such as it being damaged, lost or stolen, this may result in you not being able to claim winnings.
Simplicity If you win a prize, you will be notified directly by email and your winnings will be paid once you complete your prize claim procedure. It is your responsibility to check your ticket against the official results and if you have won, you will have to collect your winnings from an authorised store or, depending on the size of the prize, you may have to claim through an authorized agent assigned by OZ Mega Millions.
