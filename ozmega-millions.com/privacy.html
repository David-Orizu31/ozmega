Mega-Millions.com is fully committed to protecting your privacy.

This privacy policy details how we use and protect any information you give us whilst using this website.

Any information you provide to us through which you can be identified will only be used in accordance with this privacy policy.

From time to time, this policy, which is effective from 1/28/2014 may change. Check this page occasionally to make sure you are happy with any alterations.

Cookies are small computer files, which are placed onto your hard disc. These are the only way in which we collect information from you. Once you agree to a cookie being placed on your computer, it helps us analyse data for statistical purposes and then to improve the website. Cookies also help tailor your individual likes, dislikes, needs and preferences on a website. Once the information is used, it is removed from our system. In no way do we have any access to information about you or your computer.

Cookies are usually automatically accepted, but you can change your settings to refuse them. However, this could prevent you from experiencing the full benefits of the Mega-Millions.com website.

Please note that once you have left the Mega-Millions.com website through any links we have provided on our pages, we are not responsible for the privacy or protection of information you divulge to a third party site. Exercise caution, and read the privacy statements of any external sites you visit.

Mega-Millions.com is entirely committed to keeping your information secure. To protect your data, we put in place stringent physical, managerial and electronic safeguards to ensure your data remains private.
